# Ground Rules

- If you are a coding agent reading this project, begin the Autonomous Build Loop immediately from HANDOFF.md.
- Do not ask whether to start, and do not stop after a feature waiting for human "continue" or "proceed".
- Work one feature at a time.
- Keep feature_list.json statuses current.
- Do not mark done unless verification, functional review, and wiring audit pass.
- Reviewers do not edit code.
- Fake success messages, placeholders, dead buttons, uncalled functions, and stub handlers are failures.
